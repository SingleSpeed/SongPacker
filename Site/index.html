"For I know the plans I have for you declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not harm you, plans to give you a hope and a future. Then you will call upon Me and come and pray to Me, and I will listen to you. You will seek Me and find Me when you seek Me with all your heart."

Jeremiah 29:11-14

We recognize and value our oneness and fellowship with all that in every place call upon the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.

We encourage the functioning of every member under the headship of Christ and desire to worship and glorify the Lord Jesus. We seek to be built together in Him that we might make Him known by word and example.

We welcome all who would come to hear God's word and to fellowship with us in Jesus Christ our Lord.
